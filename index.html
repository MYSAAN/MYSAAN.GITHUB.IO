RAMADAN MUBARIK

written by Mysaan Mohamed

Ramadan

This year Ramadan started on the 18th of June. As usually we had time off school to celebrate the month of Ramadan with family and friends.

This ramadan I have seen many students from this school sitting in the mousque resiting quran which made me feel very happy. Also I have seen the mousque full of people which is a very rare sight in this island.

At evening almost all the men in the island gathers at the mosque to break their fast. This year there were many men at the mosque again.
